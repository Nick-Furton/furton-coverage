Guidance changes

CrowdStrike guided Q2 FY2027 revenue of $1,436.0M–$1,442.0M and non-GAAP EPS of $1.16–$1.17, and raised full-year FY2027 non-GAAP EPS guidance to $4.88–$4.96 (from $4.78–$4.90 given back in March). First-read verdict

A clean beat-and-raise print on the headline financials: revenue and non-GAAP EPS both came in above the guided range, and forward guidance was raised rather than merely reaffirmed. The one place our preview's thesis did NOT hold up is net-new ARR: we called a continuation of Q4 FY26's reacceleration ($330.7M net new ARR), but Q1 FY27 added $255.8M -- down sequentially, even though total ARR still grew 24% year-over-year to roughly $5.51 billion. The company's own framing (raising full-year net-new-ARR growth guidance) suggests management reads the underlying trend as intact despite the sequential dip, but our specific qualitative call was too optimistic about Q1 specifically.
